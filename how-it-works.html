How Nura BioHealth Works

From your first consultation to ongoing support, Nura BioHealth makes the process simple and reassuring. Here's what to expect when you start.

Step 1

Tell us about yourself

Complete a brief online form to share your health history, current medications, and goals. It only takes a few minutes, and you can choose between an asynchronous review or a live video visit.

Both options are secure, HIPAA-compliant, and designed to fit your schedule.

Step 2

Provider reviews your information

After you complete your intake form, a licensed provider reviews all the details to create a safe and effective plan that fits your goals.

Live (synchronous)

Real-time video or phone visit with your provider.

Message-based (asynchronous)

Secure online visit, where your provider reviews your information and follows up with your care plan.

Your provider will choose the format that's best for your situation, and may reach out with questions or schedule a quick live visit before prescribing.

Step 3

Medications delivered to your door

After your plan is approved, your prescription is compounded by our in-house, licensed pharmacy and shipped directly to your door.

We include all necessary supplies, and packaging is discreet to protect your privacy. We send tracking information, so you always know when to expect your package.

Typically ships within 3-5 business days with full tracking

Step 4

Ongoing support for your journey

Our relationship doesn't end when your package arrives. We check in regularly to see how you're doing, adjust dosing if needed, and answer questions so you always feel supported.

Whether it's through reminders, follow-up consults, or secure messages with your provider, you're never left on your own. We're here to help you get the best possible results at every step.
